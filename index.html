Our Story

Cooked with
Intention

Cravora was born from a simple belief — that people away from home deserve food that actually tastes like home. Based in Medchal, Hyderabad, we bridge the gap between North Indian soul food and South Indian comfort dishes.

We serve everything from Bihar's beloved Litti Chokha and rich Dal Baati to crispy Masala Dosa, fiery Chicken Schezwan Rice, and indulgent starters. Every item is prepared fresh when you order — no shortcuts.
